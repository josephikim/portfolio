Get to know me!

Hey, it's Joey Kim and I'm a fullstack web developer located in the beautiful city of South San Francisco, CA. I graduated from the Berkeley Coding Boot Camp in 2018 and later worked as a remote software engineer at Electronic Arts. Prior to that, I held technical roles at Apple Inc. and several venture-funded Silicon Valley startups.

I love building web apps from the ground up and have a good eye for frontend design. I enjoy working in diverse engineering teams and collaborating with folks from a variety of backgrounds. In my spare time I like to snowboard, play tennis and discover new music.

I'm always open to job inquiries so feel free to contact me with any roles that fit my skills and experience. Looking forward to hearing from you!
